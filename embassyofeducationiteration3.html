Ask a Question
How long does the visa process typically take?
Processing times vary by country and visa type — typically 2–8 weeks. Our documentation is always flawless to minimize delays. We maintain strong relationships with embassies across all major study destinations.
Can you help if I've had a previous visa refusal?
Absolutely. Our team of former visa officers understands exactly why refusals happen. We analyze your previous application, identify weaknesses, rebuild your profile, and craft a compelling reapplication strategy.
Is loan facilitation really free of charge?
Yes, 100%. We charge zero service fees for education loan facilitation. We work with 15+ banks and NBFCs and earn through institutional partnerships — our students never pay us for loan assistance.
What IELTS score do top universities require?
Most top universities require a minimum of 6.5 overall with no band below 6.0. Premium institutions often require 7.0–7.5. Our coaching program is designed to help students exceed these benchmarks.
Are the internships genuinely guaranteed?
Yes. Through our network of 500+ corporate partners globally, we offer guaranteed placements for enrolled students. Both paid and academic credit internships available across all major industries.
Do you provide support after the visa is approved?
Our support doesn't end at the visa stamp. We provide accommodation assistance, overseas bank account setup, cultural adaptation training, and connect you with our active alumni network in your destination country.
